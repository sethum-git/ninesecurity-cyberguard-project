Frequently Asked Questions

How fast will I get a response?

We respond to all tickets within 24 hours. Critical security issues are addressed within 1 hour.

What files can I attach?

You can attach images, PDFs, log files (.txt, .log) up to 25MB total.

Do you offer phone support?

Phone support is available for Enterprise customers. Upgrade your plan for priority phone access.

Is support available in my language?

Our primary support language is English. We offer Spanish and French support during business hours.

Is my data secure when contacting support?

All support communications are encrypted via TLS 1.3. We never ask for your password.

Can I view my ticket history?

Yes! Log into your CyberGuard account and visit the Support Portal section to view all tickets.
